  • What is Luv?

    It's an ERC20 token living in the Ethereum blockchain.
    Luv is love turned into a coin!

  • What can I do with Luv?

    Everything you can do with Love! You can give it, receive it and more!
    Luv can be sold and be bought! Directly and straightforward.
    It can also be traded for all sorts of useless stuff (just like any other ERC20 token).

  • How much Luv is there?

    At first none! Each time you buy Luv from here, new Luv will be minted and the supply will increase.
    To keeep things balanced the price will also increase.
    At some point Luv will be too expensive to buy so there is an upper limit of about 80 Billion Luv that I estimate will be minted at best.

  • How does Luv's price change?

    Luv's price depends on Ethereum price and on the currect supply of Luv tokens.
    At first the rate was 1 million LUV per 1 ETH (1000000 LUV/ETH).
    The initial rate decreases by 10 LUV/ETH for every million Luv minted.
    Therefore the price which is 1/rate increases.
