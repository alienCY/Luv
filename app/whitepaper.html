  • Introducing Luv; love tokenized.

    Luv is a mintable ERC20 token.
    The only purpose of Luv is to serve as the token of love.
    By naming it Luv we differentiate it from true Love. "Luv is the kind of love you can buy, sell or trade..".
    That sets Luv apart from the emotion and adds some benefits since it's a token in the Ethereum blockchain.
    Luv is secured in the immutable blockchain. Nobody can steal your Luv!
    It also removes uncertainty of the amount of Luv you possess since it's countable with a precision of 18 decimal places.
    At any time Luv can be obtained directly via the crowdsale's contract or through the site.

  • Crowdsale details:

    The crowdsale begins at August 1st, 2019 12:00 AM GMT.
    It ends at January 1st, 2030 12:00 AM GMT.
    Luv's initial total supply is 0 LUV. Every time you purchase Luv with your ethereum
    Luv is created, sent to your wallet and the total supply increases.
    At first the rate is 1 million LUV/ETH meaning that you get 1 million LUV for 1 ETH.
    The rate decreases by 10 for every million Luv purchased, meaning that when the total supply is 1 million LUV the rate is 999990 LUV/ETH.
    Therefore, Luv's price increases as the total supply increases.
    There is no hard cap ,however, at 100 Billion LUV the rate will be 0 LUV/ETH so that could be considered the "hard cap" even though there is no limit on the amount of Luv you can buy in one transaction.
    As a result theoritically more than 100 Billion LUV could be minted in total despite the fact that it's practically impossible to buy such a great amount of Luv -at once- to go beyond the 100 Billion LUV "hard cap" (or to buy enough Luv at all, once the rate is really low).

    • Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Luv?

    It's an ERC20 token living in the Ethereum blockchain.
    Luv is love turned into a coin!

  • What can I do with Luv?

    Everything you can do with Love! You can give it, receive it and more!
    Luv can be sold and be bought! Directly and straightforward.
    It can also be traded for all sorts of useless stuff (just like any other ERC20 token).

  • How much Luv is there?

    At first none! Each time you buy Luv from here, new Luv will be minted and the supply will increase.
    To keep things balanced the price will also increase.
    At some point Luv will be too expensive to buy so there is an upper limit of about 80 Billion Luv that I estimate will be minted at best.

  • How does Luv's price change?

    Luv's price depends on Ethereum price and on the currect supply of Luv tokens.
    At first the rate was 1 million LUV per 1 ETH (1000000 LUV/ETH).
    The initial rate decreases by 10 LUV/ETH for every million Luv minted.
    Therefore the price which is 1/rate increases.
