  • Introducing Luv; love tokenized.

    Luv is a fungible token built in the Ethereum blockchain under the ERC20 standard.
    The only purpose of Luv is to serve as the token of love; to make transactions of love fair.
    By naming it Luv we differentiate it from true love, afterall "Luv is the kind of love you can buy, sell or trade"...
    That sets Luv apart from the "pure" emotion and adds some benefits since it's a token in the Ethereum blockchain.
    Luv is secured in the immutable blockchain. Nobody can steal your Luv!
    It also removes uncertainty of the amount of Luv you possess since it's countable with a precision of 18 decimal digits.
    Therefore, by trading Luv utilizing the smart contracts' technology you are guaranteed to receive the amount of Luv you have agreed upon and keep it.

  • Crowdsale details:

    The crowdsale begins at August 20, 2019 12:00 AM GMT.
    It ends at January 1st, 2020 12:00 AM GMT.
    Luv's initial total supply is 0 LUV. Every time you purchase Luv with your ethereum
    Luv is created, sent to your wallet and the total supply increases.
    At first the rate is 1 million LUV/ETH meaning that you get 1 million LUV for 1 ETH.
    The rate decreases by 10 for every million Luv purchased, meaning that when the total supply is 1 million LUV the rate is 999990 LUV/ETH.
    Therefore, Luv's price increases as the total supply increases.
    There is no hard cap ,however, at 100 Billion LUV the rate will be 0 LUV/ETH so that could be considered the "hard cap" even though there is no limit on the amount of Luv you can buy in one transaction.
    As a result theoritically more than 100 Billion LUV could be minted in total despite the fact that it's practically impossible to buy such a great amount of Luv -at once- to go beyond the 100 Billion LUV "hard cap" (or to buy enough Luv at all, once the rate is really low).
